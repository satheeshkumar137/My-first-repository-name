Launch of AI Content Generation Challenge

We are excited to announce the launch of a new activity titled AI Content Generation Challenge. This activity aims to encourage students to explore the power of Artificial Intelligence in creating high-quality digital content.

Participants are required to research and use AI tools to generate different types of content such as articles, blog posts, social media captions, short scripts, or creative writing. Students should understand how AI tools assist in content creation and apply them effectively.

Objectives of the Activity:
• Understand the basics of AI content generation
• Explore different AI writing tools
• Create original and meaningful content using AI assistance
• Present the work clearly and professionally

Instructions:

1. Choose any AI content generation tool.
2. Generate content on a topic of your choice.
3. Edit and refine the generated content.
4. Document the tool used, prompts given, and final output.
5. Submit your work using the proper digital sharing format.

We encourage all students to actively participate and enhance their digital creativity through this challenge.
